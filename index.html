What is DAWN?

DAWN is a decentralized wireless network for delivering Internet service.
Through the protocol and hardware, property owners or residents have the opportunity to buy and sell Internet capacity in the area around them – empowering users to operate as their own Internet provider.
The decentralized nature of DAWN facilitates a trustless system of exchange that employs proof of backhaul, location, and frequency.

About Us

DAWN is a decentralized coalition of operators and users with one mission: make Internet affordable and decentralized with wireless technology.

Empowering Communities Through Decentralized Networks.
‍
At DAWN, we are on a mission to revolutionize the Internet by creating a multi-gigabit wireless network owned and operated by its users. We believe in the power of community and aim to empower individuals to be part of the Internet's future. Join us as a node host to create your own wireless coverage, earn rewards, and contribute to a decentralized network that puts control back in the hands of the people.
