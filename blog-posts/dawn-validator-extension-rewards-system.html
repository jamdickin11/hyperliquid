DAWN Validator Extension Rewards System

August 7, 2024

By participating as a DAWN Validator through the extension, you receive rewards, which are generated based on four criteria:

‍

  1. The time that you are connected
  2. Referring others to download the extension
  3. Following DAWN on X, Discord, and Telegram
  4. Participating as a validator node (coming soon!)

‍

1. The time that you are connected

Each 24 hour period has an eligible 1,440 reward points. Each user will accrue rewards based on the time that their end user device has the extension downloaded, active, and status connected.

‍

2. Referring others to download the extension

Each user has a unique referral code viewable in the extension on the “Refer a friend” page (you must be logged in to the extension to view these). You can share this code and, when others download the extension and use your unique referral code when creating an account (Referral A), you will receive bonus rewards equal to 20% of the rewards earned by the person referred in perpetuity.

In addition, when the referred user refers others who download and create an account (Referral B), your rewards will also increase by 10% of their lifetime rewards earned. Lastly, when these users refer others to the extension, you will receive a 5% bonus of the rewards they earn (Referral C).

‍

3. Following DAWN on X, Discord, and Telegram.

Follow along on X, Discord, and Telegram for updates and announcements related to DAWN. For each service you follow through the links in the extension you will receive 5,000 reward points.

‍

4. Participating as a validator node (coming soon!)

When a user participates as a proof of bandwidth validator, they will receive additional reward points, regardless of the result of the challenge. Stay tuned for more details coming soon on the validator program and rewards associated with participating.

‍

Rewards are automatically updated in the extension and you can see your balance on the main extension dashboard. In the future, there will be a browser-based dashboard for you to review your rewards accrual and sources (time based, referrals, and participation as a validator node.

‍

Don't wait: download the DAWN validator extension and start earning rewards today!

‍

Note: The rewards system is subject to change from time to time. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for full background.
